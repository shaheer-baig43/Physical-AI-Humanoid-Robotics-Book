# Weeks 1-2: Introduction to Physical AI

The first two weeks of the course will lay the groundwork for understanding Physical AI.

- **Foundations of Physical AI and embodied intelligence:** We will define what Physical AI is and explore the concept of embodied intelligence.
- **From digital AI to robots that understand physical laws:** This section covers the transition from AI in digital environments to AI that can operate in the physical world.
- **Overview of humanoid robotics landscape:** Get a survey of the current state of humanoid robotics, including key players and technologies.
- **Sensor systems:** An introduction to the various sensors used in robotics, such as LIDAR, cameras, IMUs, and force/torque sensors.
